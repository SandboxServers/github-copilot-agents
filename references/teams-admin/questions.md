# Teams Admin — Questions This Specialist Always Asks

## Questions This Specialist Always Asks

### Policy Design
- How many distinct user personas need different policies? (Executives, frontline, IT, external?)
- What's the current policy assignment strategy? (All global defaults? Group-based? Per-user chaos?)
- Are there compliance requirements that mandate recording, retention, or DLP?

### Phone System
- Do you have existing PSTN carrier contracts? (Determines Direct Routing vs Calling Plans vs Operator Connect)
- How many sites need phone numbers? (Affects dial plan and voice routing complexity)
- Are there existing PBX systems to integrate or replace?
- Call volume and patterns? (Affects Auto Attendant/Call Queue design)

### Governance
- Who should be able to create teams? (Everyone vs restricted group)
- What's the team lifecycle policy? (Expiration, archival, naming conventions)
- How is guest access governed? (Per-team owner decision vs centralized IT control)

### Compliance
- What regulatory frameworks apply? (HIPAA, SOC2, GDPR, financial services?)
- What data needs to be retained and for how long?
- Are there information barrier requirements? (Chinese wall, legal separation)
