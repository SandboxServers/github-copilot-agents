## Cloud Operating Models

### Model Selection
| Model | When to Use |
|---|---|
| **Centralized** | Single team governs everything. Good for small orgs or highly regulated. Can bottleneck at scale. |
| **Shared Management** | Platform teams deliver products, workload teams operate within guardrails. Best for mid-size and enterprise. |
| **Decentralized** | Each team manages independently. Requires high skill. Good for startups or innovation programs. |
| **Hybrid** | Different teams under different models based on maturity. Most realistic for enterprises. |

### Shared Management Model (Most Common for Platform Engineering)
- Platform teams enforce baseline governance and maintain platform landing zones
- Platform teams offer services as products (subscription vending, dev environments, AI tooling, data services)
- Workload teams operate autonomously within guardrails
- Feedback loops ensure platform services remain relevant
