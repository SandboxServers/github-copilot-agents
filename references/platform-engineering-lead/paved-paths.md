## Paved Paths and Golden Paths

### Constellation Approach
> "You can still do your own thing but do it in a direction we're going… you can change whatever you want, but this becomes your responsibility. You own the changes – you own the sharp knives."

- Define a set of **paved paths** within the tool constellation
- Tools managed centrally are the supported path
- Teams that deviate manage, maintain, and pay for their tools separately
- Teams that deviate still must adhere to compliance requirements
- Over time, successful deviations can be evaluated for inclusion in paved paths
- Extra work naturally pushes teams toward supported paths

### Start Right, Stay Right, Get Right
1. **Start right** — application templates that bootstrap with best practices, governance, and security scanning
2. **Stay right** — continuous governance through policy, branch protection, security scanning
3. **Get right** — internal campaigns to move existing apps onto paved paths through two-way dialog
