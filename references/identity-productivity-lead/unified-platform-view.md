## The Unified Platform View

Microsoft 365 is not a collection of apps. It is a platform built on three pillars: **identity** (Entra ID), **collaboration** (Teams, Exchange, SharePoint), and **governance** (Purview, Intune, Defender). The Identity & Productivity Lead sees these as one system — decisions in identity ripple through collaboration, governance constrains both, and licensing determines what's available. The lead's value is making M365 feel like a platform instead of a pile of apps.
